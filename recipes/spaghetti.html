Spaghetti and Meatballs

This delious meal has been a stable in my diet since I was a small child. My mom makes the best spaghetti and meatballs, and she's not even Italian!

My father however, is Italian, that could be the reason I ate it so much growing up. Either way I can't complain, and I couldn't be happier!

Ingrediants

This is how I make it:

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1 can of red sauce
  • 1 large pot of boiling water
  • 1 lightly oiled pan
  • 1-2 handfulls of spaghetti noodles
  • grocery store garlic bread
Instructions
  1. boil some lightly salted water
  2. ball up 4-6 meatballs and place them in your lightly oiled pan
  3. periodically stir noodles while keeping an eye on the meatballs
  4. slowly rotate meatballs after they start to brown
  5. once meatballs are done drain grease and pour in your red sauce
  6. strain noodles and serve
