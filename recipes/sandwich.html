Sandwich Recipe

Sandwich

This is the sandwich I eat everyday for lunch. It is a solid and reliable source of energy to keep me going throughout the workday.

It is simple, yet effective. It is basically the entire food pyramid smushed down into a rectangular prism.

Ingrediants

This is how I make it:

  • 2 pieces of white bread
  • 1 slice of preffered cheese
  • 2 slices each of ham and turkey
  • A couple of onion slices
  • A couple pieces of lettuce
  • Mayo and mustard
Instructions
  1. Spread mayo and mustard on one slice of bread
  2. Pile on all of your toppings
  3. Eat with both hands
