Cereal

Cereal is simple and tasty. It is a fast and easy breakfast, and also good as a midnight snack to subside your munchies.

It is sweet, crunchy, and milky. I can't think of a better invention.

Ingrediants

This is how I make it:

  • Cereal of choice
  • Milk of choice
  • Spoon of choice
Instructions
  1. Important!!!Pour cereal first
  2. Followed by the milk
  3. Eat with spoon
  4. Drink the milk after cereal is gone
